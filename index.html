About LazyRent

LazyRent is a local rental management application designed to help landlords manage property, tenants, and rental records efficiently.

Data & Privacy

All data is stored locally on the user's device. Optional synchronization is performed using Apple's private iCloud storage.

The app does not operate any backend servers and does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

App Function Availability

LazyRent provides its core features as a local management tool.

During different development and optimization stages, certain features may be adjusted or gradually introduced to improve usability and stability.

The app does not include any in-app purchases, subscriptions, payment systems, or external service activation.

关于懒虫收租

懒虫收租是一款本地运行的租务管理应用， 用于帮助房东管理房源、租客及租金记录。

数据与隐私

所有数据均存储在用户设备本地， 可选择使用 Apple 提供的 iCloud 私有空间进行同步。

应用不设任何服务器， 不收集、不上传、不共享任何用户个人数据。

功能说明

懒虫收租作为一款本地租务管理工具， 提供完整的基础管理功能。

在不同的开发与优化阶段， 部分功能可能会进行调整或逐步开放， 以提升整体使用体验与稳定性。

应用内不包含任何付费、订阅、内购或外部服务激活机制。
