About LazyRent

LazyRent is a rental management application designed to help landlords manage properties, tenants, and rental records efficiently.

Data & Privacy

All data is stored locally on the user's device. Optional synchronization is performed using Apple's private iCloud storage.

The app does not operate independent backend servers and does not collect, transmit, or share personal data with third parties.

In-App Purchases

LazyRent offers optional paid features through Apple In-App Purchases to enhance advanced management capabilities.

All payments are processed securely by Apple via the App Store. LazyRent does not access or store any payment information.

No external payment methods, third-party payment services, or off-platform purchases are supported.

关于懒虫收租

懒虫收租是一款租务管理应用， 用于帮助房东管理房源、租客及租金记录。

数据与隐私

所有数据均存储在用户设备本地， 可选择使用 Apple 提供的 iCloud 私有空间进行同步。

应用不设独立后台服务器， 不会向第三方收集、上传或共享用户的个人数据。

应用内购买

懒虫收租提供可选的付费功能， 用于解锁更高级的租务管理与统计能力。

所有付费均通过 Apple App Store 的应用内购买系统完成， 支付过程由 Apple 统一处理， 应用本身不会获取或存储任何支付信息。

应用不支持任何外部支付方式、 第三方支付平台或站外购买渠道。
